ABOUT THE GAME

THE STORY

After a mysterious incident aboard the deep space research vessel "Erebus," you awaken in darkness. The ship's AI has gone silent. The crew is missing. And something else is on board.

Your eyes damaged in the incident, you must navigate using only sound and other senses to escape the horror that hunts you.

GAMEPLAY

BLIND is an immersive first-person horror experience that challenges you to survive without sight. Use audio cues, vibrations, and limited echolocation to navigate a hostile environment.

With each sound you make, the entity draws closer. Silence becomes your greatest asset‚Äîand your greatest obstacle.

THE EXPERIENCE

BLIND pushes the boundaries of sensory horror gaming with its revolutionary audio design and haptic feedback systems.

Each playthrough is unique as the ship's layout and threat locations dynamically change, creating a new experience of terror every time.
